Smart Tools – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-11

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Smart Tools (the “App”) handles information. The App is developed and published by Vlady's Tools (the “Developer”).

1. Overview

Smart Tools is a collection of device utilities, including a flashlight, spirit level, inclinometer, compass, screen tester, lux meter, sound meter, metal detector, QR / barcode scanner with OCR, and moon phase tools. The App is designed to work locally on your device and does not require you to create an account or provide personal details.

The App itself does not collect, store or transmit personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number or contact details. Some limited information may be collected by third-party advertising services, as described below.

2. Information the App does NOT collect

The App does not collect or store:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers or contact information
  • Passwords, login details or account information
  • Precise location (GPS)
  • Contact list, calendar events or SMS messages
  • User-generated content outside of the App’s own temporary processing

3. On-device sensor data

Smart Tools uses your device’s hardware sensors to provide its utilities. Examples include:

  • Camera and flash for the flashlight, QR / barcode scanner and OCR
  • Microphone for the sound meter (decibel level)
  • Accelerometer and gyroscope for the spirit level and inclinometer
  • Magnetometer for the compass and metal detector
  • Light sensor for the lux meter

Sensor readings are used only in real time to display measurements and are not uploaded to any server by the Developer. When you close a tool, the sensor use stops.

4. Camera, photos and OCR

The App can use the camera to scan QR / barcodes or capture images for optical character recognition (OCR). You may also choose to import images from your device. These images are processed locally on your phone or tablet to extract codes or text needed for the tool’s functionality.

The Developer does not access, copy, transmit or store your photos or images on any external server. Export features (for example, saving or sharing CSV/PDF files with scan results) are fully under your control.

5. Advertising and third-party services

Smart Tools may display ads, including rewarded ads, using Google AdMob or other advertising providers. These third-party services may collect certain device information (such as advertising ID, approximate location, and usage data) in accordance with their own privacy policies.

This data is used for purposes such as ad delivery, frequency capping and measuring performance. The Developer does not receive personally identifiable information from these providers.

For more details, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Children’s privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and is not directed specifically at children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact the Developer so that such information can be removed.

7. Data retention

The App itself does not maintain online accounts or remote databases. Measurement results or scanned codes are stored only locally on your device and can be cleared from within the App (for example, by using a “clear” or “delete” option).

8. Your choices

You can control permissions such as camera, microphone or storage at any time via your device settings. If you revoke a permission, the related tool or feature may become unavailable, but the rest of the App will continue to function where possible.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

The Developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted will be regarded as acceptance of the updated policy.
