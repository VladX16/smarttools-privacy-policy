Smart Tools – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-11

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Smart Tools (the “App”) handles information. The App is developed and published by Vlady's Tools (the “Developer”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

Smart Tools is a collection of utilities that use your device’s hardware sensors, including:

  • Flashlight / torch
  • Spirit level and inclinometer
  • Compass
  • Screen test
  • Lux (light) meter
  • Sound level meter (decibels)
  • Metal detector
  • QR / barcode scanner and OCR text reader
  • Moon phases and related information

The App is designed to work locally on your device. It does not require an account or login, and the Developer does not personally identify users.

2. Personal data and PII

The Developer does not collect, store, or process information that directly identifies you as an individual (such as your name, email address, phone number, or postal address).

However, some third-party services used in the App (in particular advertising and billing services) may collect certain device information for their own purposes, as described below.

3. Data the App does NOT collect

The App, as implemented by the Developer, does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact details (beyond the support email you choose to send)
  • Passwords or account credentials
  • Contact list, SMS messages, or calendar events
  • Precise GPS location for analytics or tracking
  • Biometric data or sensitive personal categories

4. On-device sensor and tool usage

Smart Tools uses several hardware sensors and permissions to provide its features:

  • Camera & flash: flashlight / torch, QR & barcode scanning, OCR.
  • Microphone: measuring sound levels for the decibel meter.
  • Accelerometer / gyroscope: spirit level and inclinometer readings.
  • Magnetometer: compass and metal detector.
  • Light sensor: lux meter.
  • Storage access / file creation: exporting scan results to CSV or PDF files when you explicitly choose to save or share them.

Sensor readings are used only in real time to display measurements. These readings are processed locally on your device and are not sent to any server by the Developer.

5. Camera, images and OCR

For QR / barcode scanning and OCR (text recognition), the App may use the camera or images you select from your gallery. Images are processed to detect codes or extract text that you can view, edit and export.

The Developer does not upload your images, codes, or OCR results to any server. If you choose to export results (for example, as CSV or PDF) or share them with other apps, this is done under your control using your device’s standard share or save dialogs.

6. Advertising and analytics (third-party services)

The App may display ads, including rewarded ads, using third-party services such as Google AdMob. These providers may automatically collect certain information from your device, such as:

  • Mobile advertising ID (for example, Google Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (e.g., based on IP address)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • App usage information related to ad display and performance

This data collection is performed directly by the third-party provider and is subject to their own privacy policies and terms. The Developer does not have access to data that can personally identify you from these providers.

For more information, please see:
Google Privacy Policy – https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Purchases and billing

Smart Tools may offer a premium upgrade using the official Google Play Billing system. Payment details (such as credit card information) are handled by Google Play and are not visible to the Developer. The Developer receives only non-personal information (e.g., that a purchase was successfully completed).

8. Data Collection & Processing – Server-Side Verification

Purchase & Reward Verification

When you make an in-app purchase or watch a rewarded advertisement, the App performs server-side verification to prevent fraud and duplicate rewards. During this process, the App may send limited information to our verification service.

Data sent to the verification server may include:

  • Transaction ID
  • Purchase token (sent for verification; stored only as a cryptographic hash)
  • Anonymous user identifier (UUID generated by the App)
  • Package name
  • Reward metadata (reward amount / reward item)
  • Optional custom data (if used, it must not include personal information)

This data is used only to verify legitimate purchases, prevent replay attacks, and prevent duplicate ad rewards. It is not used for profiling, and it is not combined with information that would identify you as a real person.

Processing and retention

Verification is processed using Cloudflare Workers. Verification data is stored temporarily for fraud prevention and validation:

  • Rewarded ad verification: up to 30 days
  • Purchase verification records: up to 1 year

After these periods expire, the data is deleted or automatically purged.

Cloudflare logging and IP processing

Cloudflare may process IP addresses and request metadata for security, abuse prevention, and logging purposes. This information is handled according to Cloudflare’s privacy and security practices.

9. Third-Party Services

Smart Tools may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (advertising and rewarded ads)
  • Google Play Billing (purchases)
  • Google Play Developer API (purchase verification)
  • Cloudflare Workers (verification processing)

10. Data retention

The App does not maintain remote user accounts or cloud storage managed by the Developer. Measurement values and locally stored scan results remain on your device and can be cleared or deleted by you (for example, using “clear” / “delete” options in the App or by uninstalling the App).

Third-party services (such as advertising providers and Google Play) may retain data according to their own policies.

11. Your choices and controls

You can control many aspects of data and permissions, including:

  • Device permissions: You can grant or revoke camera, microphone, storage and other permissions at any time in your device’s system settings. The related tools may not function without those permissions.
  • Advertising preferences: On Android, you can reset your advertising ID or limit ad personalization in the system settings.
  • App uninstall: You can stop all use of the App and its data by uninstalling it.

12. Children’s privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and is not directed specifically at children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact the Developer so that such information can be removed if necessary.

13. International users
